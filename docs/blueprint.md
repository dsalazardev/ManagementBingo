# **App Name**: GestiónBingo

## Core Features:

- Concept and Definition Management: Teacher can input, edit, and categorize theoretical concepts and their detailed definitions, ensuring they are precisely linked to study materials.
- Dynamic Bingo Card Generation: Automatically generates and displays unique 5x5 digital bingo cards for each student with 24 randomly distributed concepts and a 'Free Space'.
- Interactive Student Card: Allows students to mark concepts on their digital card as definitions are read, providing visual feedback.
- Game Master Control Panel: A dedicated interface for the teacher to initiate game sessions, read definitions from their list, control game pacing, and monitor active players.
- Real-time Bingo Detection: Automatically detects when a student achieves a winning line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) and notifies the teacher.
- AI-Assisted Validation Tool: When a student claims Bingo, an AI-powered tool assists the game master in validating the claim by verifying if the marked concepts on the student's card correspond to definitions previously read during the game session.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: A vibrant, rich blue (#1D55E6) representing intellect and engagement, providing strong contrast for text on a light background.
- Background Color: A very light, desaturated blue-grey (#F2F4F7) to maintain focus and readability without being stark white.
- Accent Color: A warm, energetic orange (#F5A52A) to highlight interactive elements, calls to action, and convey a playful, competitive spirit.
- Headline Font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, clear, and slightly tech-inspired look. Body Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for high readability in definitions and instructional text.
- Use clean, geometric icons that visually represent management or technical concepts, enhancing clarity and complementing the app's modern aesthetic.
- Implement a clear grid-based layout for bingo cards, and distinct, organized sections for teacher and student views, emphasizing readability and efficient information processing.
- Incorporate subtle feedback animations for actions like marking a concept, and a more pronounced but smooth animation for a 'Bingo!' event, to enhance engagement without being distracting.
